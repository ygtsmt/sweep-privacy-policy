# Privacy Policy for Sweep

**Last updated:** [29 June 2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how [Mind Mons Bilişim Teknolojileri] ("we", "us",
"our") handles information in connection with the **Sweep** mobile application
(the "App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

## Summary
Sweep helps you clean up your photo gallery on your device. Your photos and
videos are processed **on your device** and are **never uploaded to us** — we do
not operate servers that receive or store your media. We use a small number of
third‑party services for ads and subscriptions, described below.

## Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect, upload, sell, or store your photos or videos.
- We do not require an account, name, email, or login to use the App.
- Your "Locked Folder" items are stored only in the App's private, sandboxed
  storage on your device and protected by your PIN or device biometrics
  (Face ID / fingerprint). We never receive them.

## Information Processed on Your Device
To work, the App reads your device photo library (with your permission) and
stores small settings locally on your device, such as: your theme and language
choice, onboarding state, and which items you have moved to the trash, reviewed,
or locked. This data stays on your device and is removed when you delete the App.

## Permissions We Request
- **Photo Library access** — to display and manage your photos and videos.
- **Face ID / Biometrics** (iOS) — to unlock the Locked Folder. Handled by the
  operating system; we never receive your biometric data.
- **Tracking (App Tracking Transparency, iOS)** — see "Advertising" below.

## Third‑Party Services
We use the following services, which may process limited data according to their
own privacy policies:

- **Google AdMob** (advertising). To show ads, Google may process device
  identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), IP address, approximate location, and
  app‑usage/ad‑interaction data. Learn more:
  https://policies.google.com/privacy and
  https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
- **RevenueCat** (subscription management). When you purchase or restore a
  subscription, RevenueCat processes the transaction, a randomly generated
  anonymous app user ID, and device/OS information to manage your entitlements.
  Learn more: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
- **Apple App Store / Google Play** process your payment for any purchases. We
  never receive your full payment details.

## Advertising & Consent
The App may show ads via Google AdMob. Depending on your region:
- **iOS:** we ask for permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt
  before any tracking for personalized ads. You can change this anytime in
  iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
- **EEA/UK and other applicable regions:** we present a consent message
  (Google's User Messaging Platform) so you can choose how your data is used
  for ads. If you do not consent, you may see non‑personalized ads.

## Purchases
Sweep may offer an optional auto‑renewing subscription ("Premium"). Purchases
are handled by the App Store / Google Play and managed via RevenueCat.
Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled in your store account
settings. We do not store your payment information.

## Data Retention & Security
Settings and locked items remain on your device until you delete them or remove
the App. Third‑party services retain data per their own policies. We take
reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of storage or
transmission is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your
jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Rights
Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR/EEA, UK, CCPA/California), you may have
rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, and to opt out of certain
processing. Because we do not maintain accounts or store your media, most data
lives on your device or with the third parties above; please also refer to their
policies. To make a request, contact us below.

## International Transfers
The third‑party providers above may process data in countries other than your
own. Those transfers are governed by their respective safeguards.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last
updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide further notice.
